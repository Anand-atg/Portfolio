About Me

Hello! I'm Anand — a passionate MIS Analyst with a knack for transforming complex data into actionable insights. I specialize in crafting automated dashboards and reports that empower decision-makers in the banking sector to act with speed and confidence. My journey in data and banking began over 2.5 years ago at CAMS, supporting SBI's operations, where I learned the value of precision, timeliness, and client trust. As I transitioned into more advanced roles at Wipro on the RAKBANK-UAE project, I discovered my passion for transforming raw data into impactful business intelligence. Over time, I became the go-to person for MIS reporting, using SQL, Power BI, and Excel to automate workflows, track key KPIs, and optimize SLA/TAT performance. I’ve successfully handled settlement operations, managed high-volume payment processing, and ensured compliance—all while driving clarity through clean, reliable data. Beyond numbers, I focus on uncovering trends and customer insights that support brand awareness and strategic decisions. For me, data isn’t just about analysis—it’s about creating real business impact. Feel free to explore my projects or connect with me on LinkedIn — let’s talk data!.
